What is this?

Curator provides a spot where you can create and organize thoughts in the form of writing and simple sketches.

Curator runs and stores data in your browser. Your thoughts never leave the browser unless you decide to store them in your Google Drive or download them to your local machine. You can work offline, and if you close your browser, you should be able to return and pick up where you left off.

That being said, the browser can forget your work if (for instance) you clear the browser data. For this reason, it is probably a good idea to save your efforts (just as you would save them in a desktop word processor).
